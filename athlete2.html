About

Becca is a junior on the Skyline cross country team and has served as team captain for both her sophomore and junior seasons. Her favorite memories are spending time with teammates at meets and team events and celebrating each other's progress. The highlight of this past year was the team winning the Holly Duane Invitational, where she set a course record for Division 1 girls. Another runner broke that record about 30 minutes later, but the performance still stands out as one of her proudest races.
