About

Zeke Lafferty is a senior on the Skyline cross country team, graduating with the class of 2026. Over four years of racing, he has steadily improved from reserve races as a freshman to competing at state and post-season meets as an upperclassman. His results include appearances at the MHSAA Division 1 Finals and the MITCA Michigan Meet of Champions, along with strong performances at league jamborees, invitationals, and regional meets.

Zeke’s season-best time of 16:26.7 came at the DeWitt PROM 5K, reflecting the hard work he put in throughout his senior year. Whether racing in varsity championship fields or reserve divisions earlier in his career, he has been a consistent performer and a key contributor to Skyline’s distance squad.
